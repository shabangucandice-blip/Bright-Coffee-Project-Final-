# Bright-Coffee-Project-Final-
Conducted a comprehensive data analysis of raw data from Bright Coffee franchise to inform strategic business decisions for incoming CEO. 
Key objectives: 
calculate total revenue and analse sales trends
identify peak customer purchase times and patterns
determine top-revenue generating products and product categories
analyse transactional data to identify peak hours and time buckets 
provide actionable insights to inform business structure, inventory management and marketing strategies.

Scope of work : Analyse raw data from bright coffee- 3 locations
                Apply data analytics techniques to extract insights and trends
                Present findings and recommendations to support informed decision-making for the incoming ceo.
